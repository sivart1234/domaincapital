The .ai Opportunity

The most valuable domain extension in a generation

The .ai top-level domain has transformed from an obscure country code for Anguilla into the de facto digital identity for the artificial intelligence industry. In less than three years, .ai has followed the trajectory of .com in the 1990s — but at a pace no one anticipated.

In 2023, .ai registrations generated over $30 million in revenue for Anguilla alone, representing more than 10% of the island's entire GDP. Premium .ai domains now routinely trade for five and six figures on secondary markets, with the highest recorded sale exceeding $1.5 million. Category-keyword .ai domains — the ones that describe entire industries — are nearly fully registered.

Every serious AI company, from seed-stage startups to Fortune 500 enterprises, now recognises that the right .ai domain is a strategic asset: for branding, for credibility, for talent acquisition, and for investor signaling. The window to secure the most valuable names is closing.

Domain Capital exists to ensure these assets end up with the organisations that will use them most effectively.
