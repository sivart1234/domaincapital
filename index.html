The Market

AI-designed antibodies are redefining pharmaceutical development

Antibody therapeutics represent the fastest-growing class of approved drugs worldwide. Artificial intelligence has compressed the discovery timeline from years to weeks.

Generative AI models now design novel, functional antibodies entirely from scratch. Multiple AI-designed antibodies have entered clinical trials, backed by billions in venture capital and the world's largest pharmaceutical companies.

Organizations including AstraZeneca, Eli Lilly, Sanofi, AbbVie, and Takeda have collectively committed billions in partnerships with AI antibody design platforms, with individual deal values exceeding $1 billion.
